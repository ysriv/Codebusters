1. Inspiration

The inspiration of TrashBuddy was drawn from the Life of "Nek Chand Ji", one who made the entire "Rock Garden" of "Chandigarh". He dedicated his entire life to make the world a cleaner place by reusing trash.

2. What it does ?

Our primary goal is "Invest Trash, Harvest Love". So, to achieve our goal we made a medium which is TrashBuddy. Basically, this is like a social media platform, where the users can interact with people of similar interest. In TrashBuddy, users can provide trash to those creative minds which can make a worth out of it. TrashBuddy is just a gateway between Trash Givers and Treasure Makers.

3. Challenges we ran into

Being novel to these kind of tasks, coming up with the idea, our team came across various challenges including GUI, implementation of Google Login etc.

4. What we learnt

Trash of someone could be a treasure for someone else. We just need to connect both the users via some kind of gateway, here TrashBuddy does the job.

5.What's next for this

With TrashBuddy, our team aims to convert Trash into Treasure. Our team seeks to expand it beyond limits.
